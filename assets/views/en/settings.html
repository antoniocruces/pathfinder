Requirements.

  • 8 MB or more of RAM. Microprocessor equal to or later than Intel Core i3.
  • The disk size is not essential, although it is suggested to have 250 or more MB free.
  • MS Windows operating system v ≥ 10, Linux kernel v ≥ 4, macOS v ≥10.
  • Browser Firefox v ≥ 50, Safari v ≥ 10 (not recommended), Chrome v ≥ 51 (recommended), Opera v ≥ 40.

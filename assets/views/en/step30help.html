IMPORTANT: in the list, PATHFINDER always shows the primary records selected by the active filter. Keep it in mind if the desired data does not appear and rectify the filter accordingly.

1. SEARCH & NAVIGATION.

Top area of the table allowing to scroll through the dataset using a slider or page by page. It also allows you to export data in CSV format, print the table and perform filtered searches on it.

When PF shows a table ase result of an analytical operation, elementary descriptive statistics data is presented on this area, as well as information on outliers, very important in the process of discovering new knowledge carried out by PF.

2. TABLE.

Ordered list of filtered data. You can alter the order of presentation by pressing alternatively on the heading of each column (it will change from least to greatest, A → Z, and vice versa).

3. SINGLE RECORD.

Clicking on a primary record title, PF will show the appropriate single record screen.

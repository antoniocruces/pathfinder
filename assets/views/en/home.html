A tool for Digital Art History.

PATHFINDER is a software development carried out by iArtHis_LAB Research Group, of the Department od Art History of the University of Málaga (Spain), as part of the projects Exhibitium FBBVA 2014 and ArtCatalogs (HAR2014-51915-P) to make available to researchers interested in the application of the digital paradigm to the study of the Humanities (Art History in particular) a versatile tool allowing quantitative analysis of qualitative information of the discipline.

In order to use PATHFINDER you must have a data file with a specific format. If you do not have one, contact iArtHis_LAB to have granted access to ExpoFinder.
